# Narendra Modi: A Comprehensive Academic Guide

### Overview
Narendra Modi is a prominent Indian politician and government official who has served as the Prime Minister of India since 2014. A senior leader of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), he previously held the position of Chief Minister of Gujarat state for over a decade. His political career is marked by significant electoral successes at both state and national levels, leading to his current third consecutive term as Prime Minister.

### Key Concepts
*   **Narendra Modi (Narendra Damodardas Modi):** Born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, India; a central figure in contemporary Indian politics.
*   **Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP):** A major political party in India, of which Narendra Modi is a senior leader.
*   **Lok Sabha:** The lower chamber of the Indian parliament, where members are directly elected by the people. Elections to the Lok Sabha determine the composition of the national government.
*   **Chief Minister:** The head of government of an Indian state, similar to a governor or premier in other political systems.
*   **Prime Minister of India:** The head of government of the Republic of India, holding the most powerful political office in the country.
*   **National Democratic Alliance (NDA):** A coalition of right-leaning political parties in India, led by the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).
*   **Gujarat State Assembly Elections:** Elections held to determine the members of the legislative assembly for the state of Gujarat, which then forms the state government.
